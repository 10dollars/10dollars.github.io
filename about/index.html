About

First thing's first. This site is heavily (heavily) indebted to Daniel Bogan, whom I've never met, but who was generous enough to post the code for his interview site, The Setup, on GitHub for anyone to use.

Okay, onward. My name is Thomas Dunlap, and I'm a designer and masters student living in Seattle, WA. I Had 10 Dollars started as a joke I made on Twitter about the domain ihadtendollars.com being the most descriptive name for most of the sites I'd make. Basically, meaning that I register domains just because they sound funny and then I try to figure out what to do with them. As soon as I made the joke I registered ihadtendollars.com. Of course, it just sat gathering dust for over a year before I figured out that I wanted to use it to show off side projects that my friends and other awesome people were making.

Once I figured out I wanted to interview people, as opposed to create some sort of directory, I knew I wanted it to be simple and structured like The Setup. To get it up and running faster, I structured it exactly like The Setup.

If you want to get in touch with me, you can always reach me on Twitter. I also answer email at whatscene@gmail.com. Feel free to send me cool projects I should know about. :)
