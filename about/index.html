About

First thing's first. This site is heavily indebted to Daniel Bogan, whom I've never met, but who was generous enough to post the code for his interview site, The Setup, on GitHub.

Okay, onward. My name is Thomas Dunlap, and I'm a designer and HCDE masters student living in Seattle, WA. I Had 10 Dollars started as a joke I made on Twitter about the domain ihadtendollars.com being the most descriptive name for most of the sites I'd make. Basically, meaning that I register domains just because they sound funny and then I try to figure out what to do with them. As soon as I made the joke I registered ihadtendollars.com. Of course, it just sat gathering dust for over a year before I figured out that I wanted to use it to show off side projects that my friends and other awesome people were making.

I love weird little projects and single serving sites. Niche communities and projects designed to scratch their creator's peculiar itch. I'm not exactly sure what this site will be yet, but I know I want it to be a place where a small in joke can have a home next to a relatively big project that could make a go at being a full time gig for a crafty developer. The web is a delightful place full of whimsy, and I'd like to shine a light on that.

If you want to get in touch with me, you can always reach me on Twitter. I also answer email at whatscene@gmail.com. Feel free to send me cool projects I should know about. :)
